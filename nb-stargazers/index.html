About NBSS

Welcome to the New Braunfels Stargazer Society - a celestial haven where the wonders of the cosmos come alive! This community for avid stargazers, astronomers, and space enthusiasts of central Texas who share a passion for exploring the mysteries of the universe. Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or a curious beginner, we invite you to join our community and embark on an astronomical journey like no other.

Our website currently has two sections: using data gathered from NASA one can enjoy the Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) for today or from any previous date, and also available are images and information gathered by a NASA rover on Mars.
